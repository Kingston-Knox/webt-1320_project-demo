Do's and Dont's of necromancy

The graves

DO pick from the older graves, they tend to revive faster if the soul has already parted.

Do NOT revive the criminals, learned this one the hard way myself.

Commands

DO give your newly revives companions simple commands, it is easier for them to understand.

Do NOT ask them to cook you food, tends to get pretty dusty.

Treatment

DO treat skeletons with respect, sometimes they are self conciouse.

Do NOT attempt to give them new skin, it just starts to rot again.
